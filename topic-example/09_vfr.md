# Volatile Facts Register — Sourdough at Home

**Project:** Sourdough at Home: A Practical Guide to Wild Yeast Baking
**Flavor:** Topic (55,000–65,000 words)

> **Note for agent:** Volatile facts are manuscript claims that may become outdated,
> are frequently contested, or depend on external sources that can change. This register
> tracks them with source citations and recheck triggers. Before any publication update,
> check all items in this register.

---

## What Makes a Fact Volatile?

A fact is volatile if:
1. It depends on a specific product, standard, or measurement that may change over time
2. It is drawn from "practitioner consensus" that may evolve as techniques develop
3. It cites a statistic from the author's own data (which may be updated with new data)
4. It involves a comparison to other books or products (competitive claims)
